What is NZ Family Travel?

NZ Family Travel is a planning resource built specifically for New Zealand families. We cover domestic NZ destinations and a growing range of popular overseas trips from NZ. Everything on this site is written with the assumption that you are travelling with kids — and that things like kid-friendly restaurants, rainy-day backups, and realistic driving distances with toddlers actually matter.

How We Research

Our content is built from a combination of:

  • First-hand NZ family travel experience — our team have collectively visited every destination on this site with their own children
  • Real price data — we check accommodation, activity, and campervan prices regularly and update our guides when things change significantly
  • Operator verification — age restrictions, rain policies, and booking requirements are confirmed directly with operators before publication
  • Community input — we are active in NZ family travel Facebook groups and incorporate real-world feedback from parents who have done these trips recently

Our Affiliate Disclosure

We earn a small commission from some bookings made through our affiliate links — this never affects our recommendations. We only link to operators we would use ourselves. You pay the same price whether or not you use our links. We are transparent about which links are affiliate links (they are marked "sponsored" in the code).

Our editorial policy: If we've had a bad experience at an attraction, or if it consistently gets poor reviews from NZ families, we won't feature it as a "must-do" — regardless of whether the operator has an affiliate programme.

Who Writes This Site?

NZ Family Travel is operated by a small team of New Zealand parents based across Auckland, Wellington, and Christchurch. We don't pretend to be professional travel journalists — we are parents who travel with kids and have worked out what actually matters (and what doesn't) through experience. Our most-visited guides are the ones that answer the questions we couldn't find good answers to when we were planning our own trips.
