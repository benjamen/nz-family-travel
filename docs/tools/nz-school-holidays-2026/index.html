NZ school holiday dates for 2026 and 2027 with crowd levels, accommodation price impacts, and expert booking advice for family travel across New Zealand.

NZ School Holidays 2026

☀️ Summer Holidays 2025–2026

~20 Dec 2025 – 29 Jan 2026 (6 weeks)
PEAK SEASON
  • Most expensive 6 weeks of the year
  • Queenstown, Rotorua, Bay of Islands packed
  • Book accommodation 4–6 months ahead
  • Activities sell out — book online in advance
  • Budget add 20–40% vs shoulder season

🍂 Autumn Holidays 2026

~9 Apr – 27 Apr 2026 (2.5 weeks, incl. Easter)
BUSY
  • Easter weekend (3–6 Apr) is the busiest — Fri/Mon are public holidays
  • Good Friday + Easter Monday mean most attractions open but with queues
  • Great weather in most of NZ — warm and settled
  • Book Easter week 2–3 months ahead
  • Week after Easter much quieter

❄️ Winter Holidays 2026

~4 Jul – 20 Jul 2026 (2.5 weeks)
QUIETER
  • Cold in most of NZ — but Queenstown is ski season peak
  • South Island indoor attractions great value
  • Te Papa, Antarctic Centre, thermal parks are ideal
  • Ski families: book Queenstown accommodation 2–3 months ahead
  • North Island destinations 30–40% cheaper than summer

🌸 Spring Holidays 2026

~26 Sep – 12 Oct 2026 (2.5 weeks)
GREAT VALUE
  • The hidden gem — warming up, not yet summer crowds
  • Rotorua and Queenstown both excellent
  • Campervan availability good, prices 15–25% below summer
  • Wildflowers in bloom in Marlborough and Canterbury
  • Recommend: this is the best-value school holiday period

☀️ Summer Holidays 2026–2027

~19 Dec 2026 – 28 Jan 2027 (6 weeks)
PEAK SEASON
  • Book NOW if travelling in this window — campervan slots fill fastest
  • January 2027 is when NZ families travel domestically en masse
  • Christmas week (21–28 Dec): quieter than New Year
  • New Year week (29 Dec–4 Jan): most expensive, busiest

🔑 Pro tip: The best value school holiday window is spring (late September/early October). Weather is warming up, campervans are available, and prices are 15–25% below summer. Rotorua is particularly good — all geothermal parks and farm shows are fully operational.
📌 Booking order: 1) Campervan (sells out first) → 2) TOP 10 holiday parks → 3) Activities with timed entry (Te Papa, Hobbiton, Waitomo) → 4) Restaurants/dining. Do steps 1–3 together when you commit to dates.
