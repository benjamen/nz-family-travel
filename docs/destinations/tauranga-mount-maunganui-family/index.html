Our Verdict

Tauranga and Mount Maunganui ('the Mount') is where New Zealand families have been going for summer holidays for generations. Mount Beach is NZ's best family beach — gentle surf, lifeguards, warm water, and excellent town amenities. It is unpretentious, warm, and has everything a family needs within walking distance.

Best for

Beach holidays, families with kids who want to surf or bodyboard, relaxed summer holidays

Not ideal for

Families who need cultural or activity-heavy itineraries — it is primarily a beach destination

Tauranga / Mount Maunganui Family FAQ

Is Mount Maunganui beach safe for young children?

Mount Beach has lifeguards from December to February and is generally one of NZ's safer surf beaches for families. Pilot Bay on the harbour side of the Mount is completely sheltered and perfect for toddlers. Young children who cannot swim confidently should stick to Pilot Bay or the shallow shore break at Mount Beach between the flags.

How far is Tauranga from Rotorua?

Just under 1 hour (62km). This makes Tauranga an excellent beach base with easy day-trip access to Rotorua's geothermal parks and farm shows. A 5-day trip could combine 3 nights at the Mount (beach focus) with 2 nights in Rotorua.

Hidden Gems — Tauranga / Mount Maunganui

Stuff the guidebooks miss — local knowledge from NZ families who've done it properly.

  • 🔍 Pilot Bay on the harbour side of the Mount — completely sheltered from surf, perfect for toddlers learning to swim, almost unknown to tourists
  • 🔍 Omanu Beach (north of Mount Beach) — same sand, slightly less crowded, popular with local families
  • 🔍 The Strand in Tauranga — the city's main waterfront, good for an evening walk with excellent cafes
  • 🔍 McLaren Falls Park — 20 mins south of Tauranga, beautiful waterfall and lake, free, rarely visited by tourists
  • 🔍 Waihi Beach — 40 mins north, beautiful and quieter than the Mount with the same sand quality
  • 🔍 Katikati Bird Gardens — small but excellent, rarely on tourist itineraries
