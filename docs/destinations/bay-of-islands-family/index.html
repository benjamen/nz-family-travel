Our Verdict

The Bay of Islands is NZ's original holiday destination and still one of the best. Paihia is the main family base — from there you can swim with dolphins, sail to deserted islands, visit the Waitangi Treaty Grounds, and enjoy some of the warmest water in the country.

Best for

Beach-loving families, water activities, NZ history

Not ideal for

Those wanting to avoid the 3.5-hour drive from Auckland

Bay of Islands Family FAQ

Can you actually swim with dolphins at the Bay of Islands?

Yes, but it depends on the dolphins cooperating. Most operators run swim/watch tours. Guaranteed sightings but not guaranteed in-water encounters. Book with Explore NZ or Fullers.

How far is Bay of Islands from Auckland?

About 3.5 hours by car via SH1. Most families drive up — no direct train. Flights take 40 mins to Kerikeri.

Is Russell worth visiting?

Yes — take the 10-minute ferry from Paihia ($15 return). Russell is NZ's first European settlement, quiet and historic. Great for lunch.
