Is Skyline Gondola & Luge Worth It for Families?

Take the gondola up Bob's Peak for panoramic views of Queenstown and Lake Wakatipu, then ride the luge down. Kids can ride solo from age 3. The restaurant at the top is good value for lunch. Budget 2–4 hours.

Pricing note: Luge rides are additional ($16–30/ride or buy a multi-ride pass)

Insider Tips

  • 💡 Go mid-week to avoid queues
  • 💡 Buy multi-ride luge passes for better value
  • 💡 The restaurant has a kids menu and great views
