Is Te Papa Museum Worth It for Families?

Te Papa is one of the best free museums in the Southern Hemisphere. Six floors of NZ history, Maori culture, natural history, and interactive exhibits designed specifically for children. The earthquake house, Gallipoli exhibition (ticketed), and giant squid are perennial kid-favourites. Allow at least 3 hours.

Pricing note: Free entry. Some special exhibitions $10–25/adult.

Insider Tips

  • 💡 Arrive when it opens at 10am — it gets crowded by midday
  • 💡 The earthquake simulator is a must for kids 7+
  • 💡 Level 2 has the best kids' interactive area
  • 💡 Free lockers available for bags and strollers
  • 💡 The rooftop cafe has great harbour views
