Is Orana Wildlife Park Worth It for Families?

Orana Park is NZ's only open-range zoo and one of the best in the Southern Hemisphere. You ride through the African Savannah enclosure by bus while lions, giraffes, and rhinos roam alongside. Hand-feed the giraffes at 2pm daily. Excellent native wildlife section too.

Pricing note: African Zone safari bus included

Insider Tips

  • 💡 Giraffe feeding at 2pm is the highlight — don't miss it
  • 💡 The native wildlife section often has kiwi activity at dusk
  • 💡 Bring a picnic — the grounds are large and beautiful
