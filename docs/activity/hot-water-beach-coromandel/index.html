Is Hot Water Beach Worth It for Families?

Hot Water Beach is genuinely one-of-a-kind. Geothermal water seeps through the sand at low tide, and you hire a spade ($5) to dig your own hot pool right on the beach. The water can reach 64°C in places so dig carefully and mix in cold seawater. It only works 2 hours either side of low tide — check hotwatertide.co.nz before you leave.

Pricing note: Beach is free. Spade hire $5. Surf shop at the car park.

Insider Tips

  • 💡 Check tide times at hotwatertide.co.nz the night before — timing is everything
  • 💡 Go on a weekday morning in shoulder season — peak summer weekends are chaotic
  • 💡 The water near the rocks can be scalding — test before sitting
  • 💡 Bring your own towel and swimwear — there are no changing facilities
  • 💡 Combine with Cathedral Cove (10 min away) for a full Coromandel day
