Is Waitangi Treaty Grounds Worth It for Families?

Waitangi is where the Treaty of Waitangi was signed in 1840, making it NZ's founding document site. The museum is world-class, there's a daily Maori cultural performance, and the grounds overlook the Bay of Islands beautifully. Under-15s are free.

Pricing note: Children under 15 always free

Insider Tips

  • 💡 The free cultural performance runs multiple times daily
  • 💡 Kids under 8 may find the history less engaging — do 1 hour and focus on the canoe and performance
  • 💡 The grounds cafe has great coffee
