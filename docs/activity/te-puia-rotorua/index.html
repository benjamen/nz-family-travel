Is Te Puia Worth It for Families?

Te Puia is Rotorua's must-do. The Pohutu Geyser (NZ's largest) erupts almost every hour, there's a kiwi house with live kiwi birds, and a working Maori arts school. Evening cultural performances available. Genuinely world-class.

Pricing note: Evening hangi & performance from $132/adult

Insider Tips

  • 💡 Go at 10:15am to catch the Pohutu Geyser eruption cycle
  • 💡 The kiwi house is dark inside — let kids' eyes adjust
  • 💡 Book evening hangi separately
