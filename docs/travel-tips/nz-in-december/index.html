NZ Family Holiday in December 2026 — What to Expect

December is the start of New Zealand's summer — long days, warm temperatures, and school holidays in the last two weeks. Early December (before the 20th) is one of NZ's best-kept secrets: summer conditions without summer crowds or prices. After the 20th, it's peak season.

Early December (1st-19th) — The Sweet Spot

  • Summer weather arrives but schools are still in — crowds are 60-70% of peak January levels
  • Campervan availability is good and prices are 15-20% below peak January rates
  • Most NZ attractions are fully operational with no spring closures
  • NZ long daylight hours already at maximum (6am-9pm+) — maximum time to sightsee
  • Beach water is warm enough for swimming in Northland, Bay of Plenty, and Coromandel by mid-December
  • Book at least 6-8 weeks ahead for holiday parks or popular activities

Late December (20th-31st) — Peak Season

  • School holidays start around December 20th — crowds surge immediately
  • Christmas week (20-25 Dec) is busy but not as extreme as New Year
  • New Year's week (27 Dec-3 Jan) is the single most expensive and crowded period in NZ
  • Queenstown and Bay of Islands are packed — accommodation prices at annual peak
  • Book New Year accommodation 4-6 months in advance minimum
  • Christmas Day: most attractions and many supermarkets closed — plan meals ahead
  • Boxing Day (26 Dec) is a public holiday — most attractions open again

Best Destinations in December

  • Nelson / Abel Tasman — NZ's sunniest region. Abel Tasman beaches are at their best in December. Not yet as crowded as January. Book water taxi 3+ weeks ahead.
  • Coromandel — Hot Water Beach and Cathedral Cove are sublime in December. Arrive before 9am for Hot Water Beach to avoid crowds.
  • Bay of Islands — warm water, dolphin cruises at their best. More accessible than January as roads are less jammed.
  • Rotorua — all geothermal parks in full operation. Farm shows running daily. Great shoulder-peak choice.
  • Wanaka — one of NZ's most beautiful December destinations. Roy's Peak has maximum daylight. Lake swimming possible by mid-December.

What to Book Now for December

  • Campervans (4-berth family vans): book 3-4 months ahead for early December, 5-6 months for late December
  • Hobbiton Movie Set: sells out for Christmas/New Year period — book when you commit to dates
  • Waitomo Glowworm Caves: timed entry books up — reserve 3-4 weeks ahead
  • Abel Tasman water taxis: 2-3 weeks ahead minimum in December
  • Bay of Islands dolphin cruises: popular departures book out — reserve when you know your dates
  • New Year's Eve accommodation near Queenstown or Rotorua: book 6+ months ahead

December Tips for NZ Families

  • UV is extreme in December — SPF50+ every day, reapply every 2 hours. Kids burn in 12-15 minutes without protection.
  • Sandflies are worst in December on the West Coast and Fiordland. DEET-based repellent is essential.
  • Pack lighter clothes than you think — December in NZ is genuinely hot. A hoodie is enough for evenings.
  • Long daylight hours (6am-9:30pm) means kids won't sleep until late — pack blackout blinds for campervans.
  • Book Christmas Day dinner ahead — most NZ restaurants are closed Christmas Day.
