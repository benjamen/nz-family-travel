Auckland May

Auckland in May is a great time to visit with kids, with mild autumn weather, fewer crowds, and a range of activities to enjoy. The average high temperature is 18°C, making it ideal for outdoor exploration. With a budget of $200-$350/day for a family of 4, you can experience the best of Auckland's beaches, islands, and attractions.

Weather and What's On

Auckland's autumn weather in May is generally mild, with average highs of 18°C and lows of 10°C. It's a great time to enjoy the city's outdoor spaces, such as the Mission Bay beach and reserve, or take a coastal walk along Tamaki Drive.

In May, Auckland hosts several events, including the Auckland Arts Festival and the Auckland Marathon. These events can attract crowds, but they also offer a fun and lively atmosphere for families to enjoy.

  • Auckland Arts Festival: 1-31 May
  • Auckland Marathon: 3 May

Best Activities for May

May is a great time to visit some of Auckland's top attractions, such as the Kelly Tarlton's SEA LIFE Aquarium, Auckland Zoo, and Sky Tower. These attractions offer a range of interactive exhibits and activities that are perfect for kids of all ages.

For outdoor enthusiasts, May is also a great time to explore Auckland's regional parks, such as Tawharanui Regional Park and Wenderholm Regional Park. These parks offer stunning scenery, walking trails, and opportunities for picnics and barbecues.

Kelly Tarlton's SEA LIFE Aquarium$120-$150

Crowd Levels and Booking Ahead

May is considered part of the shoulder season in Auckland, which means that crowd levels are generally lower than during the peak summer months. However, it's still a good idea to book ahead for popular attractions and activities, especially on weekends and during school holidays.

Some attractions, such as the Kelly Tarlton's SEA LIFE Aquarium and Auckland Zoo, offer online booking and discounts for advance purchases. It's also a good idea to check the attraction's website for any special events or closures that may affect your visit.

Practical Tips for Families

When visiting Auckland with kids, it's a good idea to plan for some downtime and flexibility in your itinerary. The city can be busy, and kids may need a break from all the activity and stimulation.

Here are some practical tips for families visiting Auckland in May:

  • Pack layers for cooler mornings and evenings
  • Bring sunscreen and hats for outdoor exploration
  • Consider purchasing a Hop-on Hop-off bus ticket for easy transportation
  • Book a family-friendly accommodation with kitchen facilities to save on food costs
  • Download the Auckland Council's app for information on parks, beaches, and events
