Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

NZ Family Travel (nzfamilytravel.co.nz) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020.

Information We Collect

Automatically collected

  • Analytics data — we use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use the site (pages visited, time on site, approximate location). This data is anonymised and does not identify you personally.
  • Server logs — standard web server logs including IP addresses, browser type, and pages requested. Logs are retained for up to 30 days.

Information you provide

  • Email contact — if you email us at hello@nzfamilytravel.co.nz, we retain your email address to respond to your query. We do not add you to any mailing list.

Cookies

We use the following cookies:

  • Google Analytics — analytics cookies (_ga, _gid) to measure site traffic. You can opt out via Google's opt-out tool.
  • Affiliate tracking — if you click an affiliate link (campervans, accommodation, activities), the destination site may set tracking cookies. We do not control these.

Affiliate Disclosure

NZ Family Travel earns commission on some referrals (campervan hire, accommodation, and activity bookings). Affiliate links do not cost you anything extra. Our destination rankings and editorial content are not influenced by commercial relationships — destinations and activities that don't pay us still appear in our comparisons.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics 4 — governed by Google's Privacy Policy
  • GitHub Pages — this site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect basic request logs per their privacy policy.

Your Rights (NZ Privacy Act 2020)

Under the Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to:

  • Request access to any personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Request deletion of personal information we hold about you

To exercise these rights, email hello@nzfamilytravel.co.nz. We will respond within 20 working days.

Contact

Privacy queries: hello@nzfamilytravel.co.nz
