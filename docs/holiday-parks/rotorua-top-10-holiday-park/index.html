Rotorua TOP 10 Holiday Park

📍 Rotorua · Rotorua & Taupo

★★★★★ 5/5 family rating

A central Rotorua park with its own natural geothermal spa pool, a five-minute walk from town. A great playground and camp kitchen make it an easy family base for exploring Rotorua's geysers and Redwoods.
