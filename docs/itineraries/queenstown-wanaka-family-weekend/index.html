Day-by-Day Plan

Day 1

Arrive Queenstown — Gondola Day

📍 Queenstown 💰 ~$150–250
  • Pick up rental car
  • Skyline Gondola & Luge (3+ hours)
  • Queenstown foreshore walk
  • Fergburger dinner (queue early)
Tips:
  • Book gondola online to skip queues
  • Buy multi-ride luge pass
🏕️ Stay: Queenstown Find accommodation →
Day 2

Jet Boat + Lake Day

📍 Queenstown 💰 ~$250–400
  • Jet boat on the Kawarau River (Queenstown Jet)
  • Lunch at Frankton Arm
  • Queenstown Gardens frisbee golf (free)
  • TSS Earnslaw Steamship sunset cruise
Tips:
  • Jet boat minimum age: 3 years. Under that, do the Steamship instead.
🏕️ Stay: Queenstown Find accommodation →
Day 3

Crown Range to Wanaka

📍 Queenstown → Wanaka 🚗 1h via Crown Range 💰 ~$60–150
  • Crown Range scenic drive (stop at top viewpoint)
  • Arrive Wanaka — Puzzling World
  • Lake Wanaka swim at Glendhu Bay
  • Cinema Paradiso evening (book ahead)
Tips:
  • Crown Range has the best views in the South Island — stop at the top
🏕️ Stay: Wanaka Find accommodation →
Day 4

Wanaka Morning + Fly Out

📍 Wanaka → Queenstown 🚗 1h to Queenstown Airport 💰 ~$30–60
  • Lake Wanaka morning walk (That Wanaka Tree photo)
  • Brunch at Federal Diner Wanaka
  • Drive to Queenstown Airport (1h)
  • Fly home
Tips:
  • Allow 2h before your flight — Queenstown Airport can be busy
🏕️ Stay: Fly out Find accommodation →

FAQ

Is 4 days enough for Queenstown and Wanaka?

Yes — 2 nights in Queenstown and 1–2 in Wanaka covers the key experiences without rushing. You could add Arrowtown (20 mins from Queenstown) as a free half-day.
