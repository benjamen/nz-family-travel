Day-by-Day Plan

Day 1

Auckland to Thames to Coromandel Town

📍 Auckland → Thames → Coromandel Town 💰 ~$150–250
  • Karangahake Gorge windows walk (SH25, 45 min, free — go through the old gold mine tunnels)
  • Arrive Coromandel Town
  • Driving Creek Railway (book 2pm session)
  • Coromandel Smoking Company for dinner
Tips:
  • Don't skip Karangahake Gorge — kids love the mine tunnels and it's free
  • Book Driving Creek Railway before you leave Auckland
🏕️ Stay: Coromandel Town holiday park or B&B Find accommodation →
Day 2

Cathedral Cove + Hahei

📍 Coromandel Town → Hahei 💰 ~$150–250
  • Cathedral Cove morning (walk 35 min each way or take the boat from Hahei)
  • Hahei Beach afternoon swim
  • Explore Hahei village
  • Fish and chips dinner
Tips:
  • Cathedral Cove walk opens at 9am — go as early as possible to beat the crowds
  • The boat from Hahei Wharf ($45 adult/$23 child return) saves the walk if you have young kids
🏕️ Stay: Hahei Holiday Resort or Hot Water Beach TOP 10 Find accommodation →
Day 3

Hot Water Beach Day

📍 Hahei / Hot Water Beach 💰 ~$80–150
  • Hot Water Beach at low tide (check hotwatertide.co.nz the night before)
  • Hire spades ($5) and dig your hot pool
  • Afternoon: return to Hahei or explore Whitianga by ferry ($5 each way)
  • Whitianga waterfront and beach
Tips:
  • The Whitianga ferry is 5 minutes and $5 — a fun mini adventure for kids
  • Water's Edge restaurant in Whitianga is excellent for a nicer dinner
🏕️ Stay: Hahei or Whitianga Find accommodation →
Day 4

Drive Back to Auckland

📍 Hahei → Auckland 💰 ~$60–100
  • Morning swim at Hahei if the weather is good
  • Stop at Miranda Shorebird Centre on the way back (thousands of godwits from Alaska, free entry)
  • Or: stop at Thames for lunch and Butterfly and Orchid Garden ($15/adult, $8/child)
  • Back in Auckland by mid-afternoon
Tips:
  • Miranda is perfect for birdwatching — tens of thousands of migratory birds from August to March
  • The Thames to Auckland stretch can be slow on Sunday afternoons in summer — leave Hahei by 2pm

FAQ

Can you do Hot Water Beach and Cathedral Cove in one day?

Yes — they're only 10 minutes apart. Do Cathedral Cove in the morning, then Hot Water Beach around low tide in the afternoon. Check tide times the day before.

Is the Coromandel drive scary?

Parts of SH25A over the Kopu-Hikuai saddle are steep and winding — take it slowly with a campervan. The main SH25 coastal route is easy. From Thames north to Coromandel Town via SH25 is a beautiful coastal road.
