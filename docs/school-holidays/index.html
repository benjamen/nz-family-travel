🗓️ When to Book — NZ School Holiday Travel

  • Campervans: Book 6–9 months ahead for Summer holidays. January and the week before Christmas go first.
  • TOP 10 Holiday Parks: Coromandel, Bay of Islands, and Kaikōura book out 3–6 months ahead for Summer.
  • Queenstown accommodation: Fills for Winter and Autumn holidays within 4–8 weeks of opening.
  • Ski lessons: Book ski school in Queenstown and Wanaka as soon as July term is confirmed — lesson slots go fast.
  • Best value timing: First and last week of Summer holidays are cheapest. Mid-January is peak pricing.
