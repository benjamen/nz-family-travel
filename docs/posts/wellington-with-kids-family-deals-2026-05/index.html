Best-Value Activities in Wellington

Wellington has a wide range of activities and attractions that are great value for families. Te Papa Museum is free to visit, and the Wellington Cable Car is a must-do experience that costs around $9 for a return ticket for adults and $4.50 for children. Zealandia Wildlife Sanctuary is another great option, with prices starting from $55 for a family of 4.

The Carter Observatory is also a great place to visit, with prices starting from $12.50 for adults and $8 for children. Wellington Zoo is another popular attraction, with prices starting from $24.50 for adults and $14.50 for children.

  • Te Papa Museum: free
  • Wellington Cable Car: $9 return for adults, $4.50 for children
  • Zealandia Wildlife Sanctuary: from $55 for a family of 4
  • Carter Observatory: from $12.50 for adults, $8 for children
  • Wellington Zoo: from $24.50 for adults, $14.50 for children

Money-Saving Tips for Families

Here are some money-saving tips for families visiting Wellington:

1. Consider visiting during the off-season, when prices for accommodations and activities are often lower. 2. Look for package deals that include accommodations and activities, which can often be cheaper than booking separately. 3. Take advantage of free activities such as visiting the Wellington Waterfront and Frank Kitts Park playground, or exploring the Botanical Garden. 4. Use public transport or walk/bike whenever possible, to save money on fuel and parking. 5. Book your activities and accommodations in advance, to take advantage of early bird discounts and avoid peak season prices.

6. Consider purchasing a Wellington City Pass, which can give you discounts and free entry to many of the city's top attractions.

  • Visit during the off-season
  • Look for package deals
  • Take advantage of free activities
  • Use public transport or walk/bike
  • Book in advance
  • Consider purchasing a Wellington City Pass

When to Book for Best Prices

It's generally best to book your accommodations and activities in Wellington as early as possible, to take advantage of early bird discounts and avoid peak season prices. However, it's also a good idea to keep an eye on prices and wait for last-minute deals or special promotions.

Bookme.co.nz is a great resource for finding discounts and deals on activities, and Discover Cars can help you compare prices on campervans and other vehicles. Booking.com is also a good place to look for deals on holiday parks and motels.
