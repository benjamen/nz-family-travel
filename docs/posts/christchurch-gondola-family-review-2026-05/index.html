Christchurch Gondola Review

The Christchurch Gondola is a must-visit attraction for families, offering breathtaking panoramic views of the city and a unique opportunity to learn about Canterbury's history. With a duration of 1-1.5 hours, it's an ideal activity for families with kids of all ages. In this review, we'll explore what it's like to visit the Christchurch Gondola with kids, its age suitability, and provide insider tips on how to make the most of your visit.

What to Expect

The Christchurch Gondola is an exciting experience for kids, with the scenic ride offering stunning views of the city and the surrounding mountains. The Time Tunnel ride is a highlight, providing a fun and interactive introduction to Canterbury's history. The gondola is suitable for kids of all ages, from 0+, making it an ideal activity for families with babies and toddlers.

However, be prepared for potential crowds and long queues, especially during peak season. The gondola ride can also be a bit bumpy, which may not be suitable for all children.

  • Stunning panoramic views of Christchurch
  • Time Tunnel ride for kids
  • Suitable for kids of all ages (0+)

Value for Money

Priced at $32 per adult and $16 per child, the Christchurch Gondola is a relatively affordable activity for families. Considering the duration of the experience and the stunning views on offer, it's good value for money. However, families with multiple children may find the cost adds up quickly.

Insider Tips

To make the most of your visit, consider arriving early in the morning or later in the afternoon to avoid the crowds. The gondola is open from 10am to 5pm, and the Time Tunnel ride is included in the ticket price. Be sure to check the weather forecast before your visit, as the views are best on a clear day.

  • Arrive early or late to avoid crowds
  • Check the weather forecast
  • Time Tunnel ride included in ticket price

Booking and Saving Money

Book your tickets online in advance to avoid queues and save time. Consider purchasing a family pass, which can work out cheaper than buying individual tickets. Keep an eye on the Christchurch Gondola website for special deals and discounts, especially during off-peak season.
