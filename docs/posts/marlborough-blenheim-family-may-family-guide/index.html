Marlborough in May

May is a fantastic time to visit Marlborough and Blenheim with kids, as the weather is mild and sunny, making it ideal for outdoor activities like dolphin watching and hiking. With a budget of $180-$300 per day for a family of four, you can enjoy the region's best attractions without breaking the bank. The best ages for visiting are 5+, with plenty of activities suitable for this age group.

Weather in May

May is a great time to visit Marlborough, with average highs of 17°C and lows of 7°C. The region experiences mild autumn weather, with some rain, but mostly sunny days. It's an ideal time for outdoor activities like hiking and water sports.

The sea temperature is still quite cool, around 12°C, but it's perfect for kayaking and swimming in the Marlborough Sounds.

  • Average high: 17°C
  • Average low: 7°C
  • Sea temperature: 12°C

What's On in May

May is a relatively quiet month in Marlborough, with fewer tourists than the peak summer months. This makes it an excellent time to visit popular attractions like the Queen Charlotte Track and the Edwin Fox Maritime Museum.

The region hosts several events in May, including the Marlborough Wine and Food Festival, which usually takes place in the first week of May.

  • Marlborough Wine and Food Festival (first week of May)
  • Queen Charlotte Track: shorter, family-friendly sections

Best Activities in May

May is an excellent time for outdoor activities in Marlborough, with mild weather and fewer crowds. Some of the best activities for families include dolphin watching, hiking, and kayaking in the Marlborough Sounds.

The Queen Charlotte Track has several shorter, family-friendly sections that are perfect for kids aged 5+.

  • Dolphin watching cruise ($80-$120 per person)
  • Hiking the Queen Charlotte Track (free, but some sections may require a fee)
  • Kayaking in the Marlborough Sounds ($40-$80 per person)

Practical Tips for Families

When visiting Marlborough with kids in May, it's essential to pack layers for cooler mornings and evenings. Don't forget to bring sunscreen, hats, and comfortable shoes for outdoor activities.

Book your accommodations and popular attractions in advance to avoid crowds and availability issues.

  • Pack layers for cooler mornings and evenings
  • Bring sunscreen, hats, and comfortable shoes
  • Book accommodations and attractions in advance
  • Check the weather forecast before heading out
  • Consider purchasing a family pass for popular attractions

Crowd Levels and Booking Ahead

May is considered the shoulder season in Marlborough, with fewer tourists than the peak summer months. However, it's still essential to book popular attractions and accommodations in advance to avoid availability issues.

Some popular attractions, like the dolphin watching cruise, may require booking at least a week in advance.

  • Book popular attractions at least a week in advance
  • Consider visiting during the week instead of weekends
  • Check for any special deals or discounts for families
