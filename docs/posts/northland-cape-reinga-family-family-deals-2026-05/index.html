Northland Family Deals

Looking for a family-friendly destination in New Zealand? Northland is a great choice, with its beautiful beaches, ancient kauri forests, and rich Maori history. With the right deals and discounts, you can have a fantastic holiday without breaking the bank. Here are the best family deals in Northland for May 2026.

Best-Value Activities

Northland is home to many amazing activities that are perfect for families. Some of the best-value activities include visiting the Kauri Museum in Matakohe, exploring the Waitangi Treaty Grounds, and sandboarding down the massive dunes at Ninety Mile Beach.

These activities are not only fun, but also educational and culturally significant. And with Bookme.co.nz's activity discounts, you can save even more on these already affordable attractions.

Money-Saving Tips for Families

To make the most of your family holiday in Northland, here are some money-saving tips to keep in mind. Firstly, consider visiting outside of peak season to avoid the crowds and save on accommodation and activities.

Secondly, take advantage of free activities like the Cape Reinga walkway, Ninety Mile Beach driving, and the Mangawhai Heads beach walk and estuary. These activities are not only free, but also offer stunning scenery and a chance to connect with nature.

  • Visit outside of peak season to save on accommodation and activities
  • Take advantage of free activities like the Cape Reinga walkway and Ninety Mile Beach driving
  • Pack a picnic lunch instead of eating out
  • Use public transport or walk/bike whenever possible
  • Book your accommodation and activities in advance to secure the best prices

When to Book for Best Prices

To get the best prices on your Northland family holiday, it's essential to book in advance. This is especially true for popular activities and accommodation, which can sell out quickly during peak season.

By booking early, you can also take advantage of early bird discounts and other special offers. And with Bookme.co.nz's activity discounts and Discover Cars' campervan deals, you can save even more on your holiday.
