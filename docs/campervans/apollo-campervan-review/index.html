Our Verdict

Worth it for 14+ day trips where the premium daily cost is amortised. For 7-day trips, consider Maui or Britz instead.

Best for

Families who want maximum comfort on longer trips (14+ days)

Not ideal for

Budget travellers or short trips

✓ Pros

  • Newest fleet — vehicles typically less than 3 years old
  • Best kitchen facilities including oven
  • Superior comfort and interior fit-out
  • Excellent 24/7 support and assistance
  • Shower, toilet, and heating in all vans

✗ Cons

  • Most expensive brand in NZ
  • Minimum hire periods often apply
  • Large motorhomes harder to park in small towns
