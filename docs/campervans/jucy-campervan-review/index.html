Our Verdict

Best value campervan hire in NZ. The 4-berth Cheeky works for small families; the Big Kahuna gives families of 4 breathing room.

Best for

Couples or families of 3 on a budget

Not ideal for

Families of 4+ needing space

✓ Pros

  • Most affordable major campervan brand in NZ
  • One-way rentals available (Auckland to Queenstown)
  • Good network of pickup locations
  • Friendly, young staff

✗ Cons

  • Older fleet — some vans showing wear
  • Customer service can be inconsistent
  • Limited storage in smaller vans
  • Not ideal for families of 4 in the 2-berth
