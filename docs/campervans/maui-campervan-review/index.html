Our Verdict

The best balance of quality and price for families. The Cascade 4-berth is the most popular family choice.

Best for

Families of 4 who want comfort and reliability

Not ideal for

Budget travellers — Maui is 30–50% more expensive than Jucy

✓ Pros

  • Newer, more reliable fleet than budget operators
  • Better customer support and 24/7 roadside assistance
  • More spacious vans with better layouts
  • Kitchen equipment included
  • One-way rentals available

✗ Cons

  • Significantly more expensive than Jucy/Mighty
  • Excess insurance can add $20–50/day
  • Peak season availability books out fast
